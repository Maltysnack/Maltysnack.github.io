Red
Rebels.

A cause worth having. A colour worth wearing. An enemy worth defeating. The Red Rebels are not a club. They are a conviction.

⚠ The Blue Bandits are cowards. They always have been. Remain vigilant.
Tenet I
The Mission is Constant

There is no off-season. No ceasefire. No moment when it is acceptable to lower your guard against the Blue Bandits and their cowardly scheming. A Red Rebel is always watching, always ready, always aware that the Bandits are looking for any crack to exploit. Vigilance is not paranoia — it is simply what the situation demands.

Tenet II
Loyalty is Non-Negotiable

A fellow Red Rebel is owed your full faith. You do not question their motives, you do not leave them exposed, and you do not negotiate their interests away for convenience. The Bandits win through division. We win through solidarity. If a Rebel is in the field, you are in the field with them.

Tenet III
Best Them in Every Arena

Not just the obvious ones. The Blue Bandits should lose on the scoreboard, in the argument, in the room, and in the record books. Every contest counts. Mediocrity is a Bandit gift we refuse to accept. When a Red Rebel competes, they compete to win — and they win in a way the Bandits cannot ignore or explain away.

Tenet IV
Thwart Them at Every Turn

If the Bandits have a plan, your job is to know it before they execute it. If they have a moment, your job is to take it from them. This is not pettiness — this is strategic excellence. A Blue Bandit victory, no matter how small, is a failure of our vigilance. We thwart not out of spite, but out of principle.

Tenet V
Red is a Standard, Not Just a Colour

You don't wear red by accident. Wearing it means something. It means you have chosen a side, understood what that side stands for, and decided it was worth the opposition. Do not embarrass the colour. Carry it with conviction, or not at all.

Neutral Parties

Not everyone is against us, but not everyone is with us either. The following factions act in their own interests and may at any moment drift blue. Relationships can be built slowly, cautiously, and with open eyes.

🌸 Pink Panthers

Sympathetic by instinct but unpredictable under pressure. Handle with warmth, not dependency.

💚 Green Goblins

Opportunistic and sharp. They'll work with you when it suits them. Make sure it suits them more than it suits the Bandits.

🟠 Oranges

Principled but self-interested. Earn their respect through results rather than rhetoric.

🍋 Lemon Lickers

Volatile. They may float blue without warning. Treat as tentative allies, never assumed ones.
