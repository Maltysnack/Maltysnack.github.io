What the sheet tracks
HP + live bar
Click to edit. Bar fills proportionally. Temp HP separate.
Spell slots
Pip toggles. Click a spell to cast — auto-consumes lowest available slot.
Concentration
Set automatically when you cast a C spell. Prompts before overwriting.
Class resources
Lay on Hands pool, Channel Divinity, Divine Sense, Chef Treats — all pip-tracked.
Active buffs
Add name + effect during play. Remove when expired.
Short / Long rest
One button restores the right things. Scrolls remain consumed permanently.
Triton spells + scrolls
Fog Cloud, Gust of Wind, Wall of Water (1/long). Dimension Door, Charm Monster, Counterspell scrolls.
Export / Import
JSON backup. Transfer state between devices. Reset to defaults.
