Roast Chicken Pearl Couscous Salad

50 min3–4 servingsSalad · Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in skin-on
  • 200g pearl couscous
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 100g baby spinach
  • Handful cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cucumber, diced
  • 50g feta, crumbled
  • Fresh mint and parsley
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Mix paprika, cumin, half the garlic, and 1 tbsp olive oil into a paste. Rub all over chicken. Season generously. Roast 35–40 min until skin is crisp and deep golden.
  2. While chicken rests, cook pearl couscous in salted boiling water for 8–10 min until tender but with a slight bite. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  3. Make dressing: whisk together lemon juice, lemon zest, remaining garlic, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Toss couscous with dressing while still warm. Add spinach, tomatoes, cucumber, and most of the herbs. Toss.
  5. Pull chicken off the bone into large pieces and lay over the salad. Scatter feta and remaining herbs. Serve at room temperature.

Chicken Mexican Burrito Bowl

30 min2 servingsMexican · Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp each: cumin, paprika, garlic powder, oregano
  • Pinch cayenne
  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 can corn kernels, drained
  • 2 limes
  • Handful fresh coriander
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Salsa, to serve
  • Salt, olive oil

Method

  1. Mix all spices with a good pinch of salt and 1 tbsp olive oil. Coat chicken thoroughly. Leave 10 min if you have time.
  2. Cook rice. When done, stir through lime juice and chopped coriander.
  3. Sear chicken in a hot pan 5–6 min per side until cooked through and charred at the edges. Rest 5 min then slice.
  4. Warm beans and corn separately in a small pan. Season.
  5. Build the bowl: rice base, beans, corn, sliced chicken, avocado. Add sour cream, salsa, and extra coriander. Finish with a lime wedge.

Beef Steak Stir Fry

25 min2 servingsBeef · Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 350g rump or sirloin steak, sliced thin against the grain
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 thumb ginger, julienned
  • 1 red capsicum, sliced
  • 1 head broccolini
  • 3 spring onions, cut into batons
  • Steamed rice, to serve
  • Sesame seeds, to finish

Method

  1. Combine soy, oyster sauce, hoisin, sesame oil, and cornflour. Toss with beef and set aside 10 min.
  2. Get a wok or heavy pan ripping hot — this is non-negotiable. Add oil and cook beef in a single layer in batches, 1–2 min per side. Remove and rest.
  3. In the same pan, stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds. Add capsicum and broccolini, cook 3–4 min, keeping the veg with some crunch.
  4. Return beef. Add spring onions and a splash of water if the sauce is too thick. Toss everything together and serve immediately over rice, scattered with sesame seeds.
