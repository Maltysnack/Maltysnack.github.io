How it works
01
Host creates a session
Pick your streaming services (Netflix, Disney+, Stan…) and region. A swipe pool of ~40 films is seeded from what's actually available to you. A short session code like WOLF-42 is generated — share it with your group.
02
Everyone swipes in private
No accounts, no login — friends just enter the code and join. Each person swipes right to like, left to pass. Long-press a card for the synopsis, cast, IMDb rating, and streaming info. Can't find something? Search any film and add it to the pool.
03
A match appears for everyone at once
When a film is liked by enough people (default: everyone), a match screen fires simultaneously on all devices via live sync. Nobody knew who wanted what until this moment. That's the point.
04
Still stuck? Shortlist vote
If the group can't match, the host triggers a structured resolution flow. The highest-scored films go to a shortlist round with nominations, vetoes, and a final vote. Ties go to a spin wheel — outcome is committed server-side before the animation plays.
🎯
Genuine group consensus
The like threshold is configurable — 100% (everyone agrees) down to 50% (majority rules). Multiple simultaneous matches resolve by score; exact ties show all winners.
Tiebreaker flow
Phase 1
Nominate
Anyone can add a film from outside the scored shortlist.
Phase 2
Veto
Each person gets one veto. Films with the most vetoes are eliminated.
Phase 3
Vote
Weighted votes; survivors proceed. Repeats if no winner emerges.
Phase 4
Spin
True tie triggers a spin wheel. Winner committed before the animation plays.
