The problem

Start with a row of tiles in two colours plus one blank, arranged at random. On every turn, one tile moves into the blank space. There are three ways a tile can move, each with its own cost:

  • Slide cost 1 an adjacent tile shifts into the blank
  • Hop one cost 2 a tile jumps over one tile to land in the blank
  • Hop two cost 3 a tile jumps over two tiles to land in the blank

The goal is to end up with every tile of the first colour to the left of every tile of the second colour. The blank can finish anywhere. Pick an algorithm below; the page finds a path and animates it.
