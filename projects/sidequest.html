SideQuest

📱 iPhone · iOS
Status In design
Platform iOS

SideQuest is a lightweight daily habit and goal tracker, built around the idea that small wins matter. Set daily or weekly sidequests — drink more water, go outside, stretch for five minutes — and get gentle nudges when you haven't ticked them off. Complete a quest and earn XP. Nothing punishing, nothing streaks-or-die. Just quiet, encouraging progress. There's also a Missions area that works like a clean todo list for slightly bigger tasks that don't fit the daily rhythm. The whole thing should feel like a cosy game that happens to make your actual life a bit better.

⚔️
Sidequests

Daily and weekly micro-goals you set yourself. Exercise, water, sunlight, whatever your thing is. Repeatable, lightweight, trackable.

✅
Missions

A clean todo-list area for bigger, one-off tasks. Separate from your daily habits but part of the same system.

⭐
XP + Progress

Finish a quest and earn XP. Nothing intense. Just a small satisfying thing that says: you did the thing.

🔔
Gentle reminders

Cute, non-aggressive nudges when you haven't done a quest yet. No guilt. Just a little hey.
