What the model thinks will happen. Bracket and group stage from 10,000 Monte Carlo runs. Single sim shows one random tournament with scores. Probability each team reaches a given round, averaged across all Monte Carlo runs. Cells are coloured per column: the strongest team in each round sets the deepest gold, others scale from there. Predicted top scorers, ranked by expected WC goals. Each player's 2025-26 club goals-per-match rate is multiplied by their team's expected tournament matches and a position weight (forwards weighted highest). Coverage grows as more WC squads are announced and more club season pages are scraped; players whose clubs do not yet have season stats are excluded.
