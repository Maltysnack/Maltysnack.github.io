World Cup 2026.

A model that simulates the 2026 World Cup and reports back what it thinks will happen. Pre-tournament bracket, per-match predictions during the tournament, refined as we learn.

Kickoff: 11 June 2026. Source on GitHub.

Championship odds

How often each team wins the cup across 10,000 simulated tournaments. Probabilities are real, the deterministic bracket above just shows who would win if every match went to the favourite.

How far each team is likely to go

Probability each team reaches a given round. Teams flagged dark horse are outside the top eight by raw rating but reach the semis in more than 8% of simulations.

Group stage

Predicted final standings. Top two from each group plus the eight best third-placed teams advance.
