Pasta Aglio e Olio

20 min 2 servings Quick · Pasta
Add photo here

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 6 garlic cloves, sliced thin
  • 60ml good olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • Large handful flat-leaf parsley
  • Salt, to taste
  • Parmesan, to serve

Method

  1. Boil pasta in heavily salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, gently fry garlic in olive oil over medium-low heat until lightly golden — 4–5 min. Don't rush it. Add chilli flakes for the last minute.
  3. Add drained pasta straight to the pan. Splash in a bit of pasta water and toss until glossy and saucy.
  4. Off heat, add parsley and toss again. Adjust salt. Serve with parmesan.

Garlic Butter Salmon

20 min 2 servings Quick · Meat
Add photo here

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets, skin-on
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 40g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh dill or parsley

Method

  1. Pat salmon dry. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. Place salmon skin-side down, press gently for 10 seconds, then leave alone for 4 min until skin is crisp.
  3. Flip, reduce heat to medium. Add butter and garlic. Baste the fish with the butter for 2–3 min.
  4. Squeeze over lemon juice, scatter herbs, serve immediately.

Banana Bread

70 min 1 loaf Baking
Add photo here

Ingredients

  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • 75g melted butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 190g plain flour

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease a loaf tin.
  2. Mash bananas thoroughly in a bowl. Stir in melted butter.
  3. Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla. Add baking soda and salt. Fold in flour until just combined — don't overwork it.
  4. Pour into tin. Bake 60–65 min until a skewer comes out clean. Cool in the tin for 10 min before turning out.

Chicken Stir Fry

25 min 2 servings Quick · Meat
Add photo here

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 thumb ginger, grated
  • Mixed veg (capsicum, broccolini, snap peas)
  • Steamed rice, to serve

Method

  1. Toss chicken in soy, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornflour. Leave 10 min.
  2. Heat a wok or large pan over high heat until smoking. Add oil, then cook chicken in batches — don't overcrowd. Remove once golden and cooked through.
  3. In the same pan, stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 sec. Add veg and toss 2–3 min.
  4. Return chicken. Add a splash of water if dry. Toss everything together and serve over rice.

Shakshuka

30 min 2–3 servings Quick
Add photo here

Ingredients

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
  • 1 brown onion, diced
  • 1 red capsicum, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt, feta, bread to serve

Method

  1. Soften onion and capsicum in olive oil over medium heat, 8 min. Add garlic and spices, cook 1 min.
  2. Pour in tomatoes. Season generously. Simmer 10 min until sauce thickens slightly.
  3. Make small wells in the sauce and crack in eggs. Cover and cook 5–8 min depending on how set you like the yolks.
  4. Scatter feta, serve straight from the pan with crusty bread.
